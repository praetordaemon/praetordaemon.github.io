Making the Most of your Block.

These dynamic learning spaces allow for the next level of technology based learning. The 50" Samsung Tv comes with HDMI dongles to allow students to connect, present, and explain their work to any size group. There is also a seating area with a view that allows students to finish their current venture, or brainstorm a new project on the blackboards that accompany each space. The Pod environment allows for a laid back learning experience that serves to benefit the students, outside of the classroom.

ClassroomCEO.

Classroom CEO, a special program at Oxford High School sponsored by the Board of Education, has kicked off to a great start. The goal is to encourage promising students to enter the workforce prepared for the issues they are going to face, with the tools to find cutting edge proactive solutions. The conversations focus on how the students can help make Oxford High School a positive role-model for the surrounding regions, and a rising star to other school systems through OHS's STEM programs and classes. Important visitors such as Oxford City School's Board of Education members, as well as Mayor Alton Craft, sat down with students from all grade levels, and discussed the real world issues that they are confronted with daily, and guiding the students in their thinking process to find practical solutions.

Teachers.

Students are not the only ones who can enjoy the environment of The Connection, as several Professional Development meetings are also held in the new learning space. Teachers can use The Connection to have cooperative meetings, discuss and analyze teaching strategies, as well as go over the impact on students and interpret student data. Teachers use The Connection to collaborate on units and develop plans to reach all students. They combine their individual knowledge in teaching to innovate brand new ideas. These ideas consist of different methods that improve the studentsâ€™ understanding. By improving their understanding, the students can apply their learning to actual real-world situations that they encounter in their daily life such as collaborating with their coworkers on a project, or solving problems that will benefit many others.

GlassClassroom.

The glass classroom is another valuable asset to the innovative complex. Students are given a large space to collaborate, illustrate ideas, and communicate with other students, in a closed off space from the third floor. Here, they can concentrate on an idea separate from other groups of students. The one-way glass allows the students to work, without distractions, while teachers, viewers, and administrators can observe the student. Watching the students, teachers and educators can learn from the students, connecting to spread ideas and grow. Within this open space, students can take advantage of the technology to present and collaborate electronically.
