Specialized Utilization of our Spaces.

These dynamic learning spaces allow for the next level of technology based learning. The 50" Samsung Tv comes with HDMI dongles to allow students to connect, present, and explain their work to any size group. There is also a seating area with a view that allows students to finish their current venture, or brainstorm a new project on the blackboards that accompany each space. The Pod environment allows for a laid back learning experience that serves to benefit the students, outside of the classroom.
