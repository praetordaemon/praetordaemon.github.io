Specialized Utilization of our Spaces.

Our revolutionary robotic designing begins and ends with our teams. Each one plays an integral role in the final product, and as such, each is worthy of its own spot. The product being marketed here is derivative of the cohesion of the efforts of each team. No one team is more important than the other, and it is through this unparallelled level of cooperation that the final result is achieved. Each of our special maker spaces is equipped with a number of fantastic elemen

Marketing.

The marketing team was tasked with spreading the word of our designs throughout the budding robotic-aided farming market. This was achieved through candid meetings between the marketing team and local leaders of the agricultural industry. This partnership between our team industry was integral to the success of the team.

Design.

The design team was tasked with giving the construction team a series of designs spanning from the booth to the robot itself. The majority of this was accomplished by applying the industry standards of 3-Dimensional AutoCAD software. Thus, after a multitude of analysis' of each structure, the build team could rest assured that the creations they were building would have no trouble withstanding the pressure it would inevitbaly placed under.

Construction.

The construction teams' singular focus was the creation of the impliments of the product. Anything from a booth design, to the robot itself, the build team was more than happy to oblige. Using cutting edge industrial standards, the construction team surpassed even the expectations of the design team.

Graphic Arts.

The graphic arts department was tasked with the creation of anything requiring the skills derived from the afore-mentioned major. Everything from banners, to websites coding and creation, to font-facing; all were challenges met by the graphic arts department.

Presentation Team

The presentation team is the face of Yellow Jacket Industries. This group represents the full span of the team, as representatives from all around the organization are brought together to not only head up the communications between groups, but further, to eloquently translate the work of each group into a finalized product.

Programming Team.

The programming team's singular focus was supplying the design and build team with programs which would successfully result in a mobile and functional robot.
